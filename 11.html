TYPHOID

Typhoid fever, also known simply as typhoid, is a bacterial infection due to specific type of Salmonella that causes symptoms.




Symptoms 

1.May vary from mild to severe, and usually begin 6 to 30 days after exposure.

2.Often there is a gradual onset of a high 

3.Fever over several days.

4.This is commonly accompanied by weakness, abdominal pain, constipation, headaches, and mild vomiting.

5.Some people develop a skin rash with rose colored spots.
